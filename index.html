Countdown!

In Countdown, 5 given numbers are presented along with a Target number. Your goal: combine some or all of the given numbers with the mathematical operations of addition, subtraction, or multiplication in order to get the Target number.


The given numbers can be Big (25, 50, 75, or 100) or Small (1-10). You get to choose how many of the 5 numbers will be Big. The rest will be Small.
